CLIP (Contrastive Language–Image Pretraining) features: embedding generation for semantic search and per-photo visual concept tagging.

🔍 Compute CLIP Embeddings

Running…

Compute CLIP ViT-L/14 image embeddings for all assets. These embeddings enable semantic search — finding photos by describing their content in natural language (e.g. "sunset over the ocean", "birthday cake") — and power the CLIP tag cloud shown on each photo's detail page. Requires the same ~900 MB CLIP model used by the aesthetic and CLIP-IQA scorers.

Custom Tag Terms

Add your own tagging terms to the CLIP vocabulary. These terms are matched against each photo's CLIP embedding and displayed alongside the predefined words in the CLIP tab of the photo detail view — but only when the photo has a computed CLIP embedding. Terms are case-sensitive and must be unique.
